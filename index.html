Why should donate blood?

Our nation requires 4 Crore Units of Blood while only 40 lakh units are available.
 Every two seconds someone needs Blood. There is no substitute for Human Blood. 
 It cannot be manufactured. Blood donation is an extremely noble deed, yet there 
 is a scarcity of regular donors across India. We focus on creating & expanding a
  virtual army of blood donating volunteers who could be searched and contacted by
   family / care givers of a patient in times of need.
